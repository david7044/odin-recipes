Sesame Peanut Noodles

This easy to make snack is a great source of protein.

It's also easy to make when you are low on energy!

Ingredients

  • Noodle shape of your choice
  • Seasame oil
  • Peanut butter
  • Garlic
  • chili paste

Steps

  1. Mince the garlic
  2. Lightly fry the garlic in sesame oil. Add the chili paste
  3. Add a half cup of peanut butter. If the brand you've chosen is particularly thick or chunky, add a small amount of warm water too.
  4. Stir rapidly until mixed, then set aside.
  5. Cook noodles according to package directions, less 30 seconds.
  6. Fold the sauce into the noodles and cook on low heat until finished. Serve with chives if desired.
