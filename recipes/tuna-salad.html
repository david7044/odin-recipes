Tuna Salad

This classic dish can be served on it's own or as the filling for a sandwich.

This variant includes crisp green apples, but these can be replaced with cucumbers or other vegetables to taste.

Ingredients

  • canned tuna in water
  • light mayo
  • 1 green apple
  • lemon juice

Steps

  1. Drain the tuna and flake it with a fork.
  2. Dice the apple and toss it with a mixture of one tsp lemon juice and 1 cup water. Drain.
  3. Toss the tuna and apple in the light mayo until consistent.
